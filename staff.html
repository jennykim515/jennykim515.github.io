Staff and Office Hours

In order to get help from a CTA for virtual office hours:

  1. Check the calendar to see if online office hours are being held at this time. If they are not, please do not clog up the queue. Your submission will be ignored.
  2. Start a Zoom meeting by going to https://usc.zoom.us, from the Meeting tabs click on Personal Room, and then Start.
  3. Enter your name and Zoom meeting info in the Google form available at https://tinyurl.com/itp115online
    4. You can see how many students are in the queue at https://tinyurl.com/itp115ohqueue
